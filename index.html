Background Information

We scanned through the Historic Timeline to help our design decisions and finding. Our data is a subset of the original data all events may not be captured in the final visualization. Countries and year of few major events are mentioned below:
2000 to 2001 - Columbian Conflict between Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia (FARC), the National Liberation Army (ELN) and the Popular Liberation Army (EPL)
2003 to 2011 - Iraq War
2008 - Terrorists attack several sites in Mumbai, India; sieges end three days later with more than 170 dead and 300 wounded; surviving attacker says Lashkar-e-Tayyiba responsible.
2009 – Series terrorist events in Pakistan, India & Afghanistan
2010 – Series of terrorist events in Pakistan , India
2013 - United States – Boston Marathon bombing
2014, 2015, 2016, 2017 - Iraq Civil War
2001 to 2021 - Afghanistan War
